# Sprint 159 - Global Policy Management

Adds a tenant-scoped policy library, immutable version lineage, review and approval workflow, controlled publication, employee acknowledgements and attestations, policy exceptions, expiration reminders, and governance metrics.

Endpoints: `/api/v1/governance/policies`, `/api/v1/governance/policy-versions`, `/api/v1/governance/policy-acknowledgements`, `/api/v1/governance/policy-exceptions`, and `/api/v1/governance/policy-metrics`.
