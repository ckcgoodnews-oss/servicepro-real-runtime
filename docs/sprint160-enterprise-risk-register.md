# Sprint 160 - Enterprise Risk Register

Adds a tenant-scoped risk catalog and taxonomy, inherent and residual scoring, ownership, KRIs and breach detection, treatment plans, heat maps, and risk dashboard metrics.

Endpoints: `/api/v1/governance/risks`, `/api/v1/governance/risk-kris`, `/api/v1/governance/risk-treatments`, and `/api/v1/governance/risk-metrics`.
